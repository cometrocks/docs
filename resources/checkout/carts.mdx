## Carts

Shopping Carts help you gather products from different merchants and handle their specific checkout processes for you.

## Bags

Bags are linked to the shop systems of merchants (such as Shopify, Magento) and keep track of their products, shipping methods, and coupon codes.

## Flow

To complete your purchase, follow these steps:

1. Create a cart.
2. Add or remove products as needed.
3. Provide shipping and billing addresses.
4. Generate a payment intent.
5. Submit your cart to finalize the purchase.
